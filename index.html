The Meaning of "React"

The word "React" signifies a response to an event, situation, or stimulus. In different contexts, it takes on various meanings:

- In **Web Development**, it refers to the React.js library used for building interactive UIs.
- In **Psychology**, it refers to how humans emotionally or physically respond to stimuli.
- In **Chemistry**, it describes the interaction between substances that result in a new compound.
